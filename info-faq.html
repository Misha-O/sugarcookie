What are the ingredients?

The sugar paste is made of just three simple ingredients: sugar, lemon juice, and water. There's nothing inorganic in the sugar paste, it tastes like a citrusy cotton candy and it's edible.

Does it hurt?

Sugaring does not stick to live skin cells—only to the hair and dead skin cells—which means less irritation and discomfort, therefore many people find it less painful than waxing.

Do you provide sugaring services for man?

No, only ladies, sorry.

Can my hair be too long to sugar?

Let us take care of it. If you're lacking landscaping, don't worry! We have trimmers and sugar paste to prepare you for the service.

I am on my period. Can I still come in?

You are more than welcome to come in, just make sure to use a tampon. Keep in mind, you may find that you are more sensitive than normal due to inflammatory tissue and an increase of hormones.

How often should I have sugaring?

For best results, it is recommended to book every 4-6 weeks.

Can I have sex after my appointment?

Yes, however, friction on the areas that were treated may cause some irritation.

Does sugaring regularly lead to permanent hair loss?

There is no guarantee and each person is different. Sugaring removes the hair from the root, which in the long run can no longer produce hair.

How is the sugar paste applied?

Your own personal, individually proportioned amount is applied and removed by hand. No double dipping!

Can it burn my skin?

The paste is about body temperature, making it impossible to burn your skin.
