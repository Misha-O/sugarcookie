Preparing for your visit
  • Please come with clean dry skin. Avoid using any powders, cream or lotions to the area being sugared.
  • For best results hair should be at least a 1/4 of an inch long.
  • Try to avoid caffeine, alcohol or other stimulants prior, they can cause water retention and cause your skin to be more sensitive.
  • Note that you may be more sensitive if you are close to your menstrual cycle or are pregnant.
  • On the day of treatment, get excited; your about to start a very hair-free part of your life!

If you currently…

  • Razor/Shave – Allow at least 10-14 days of regrowth before getting sugared.
  • Wax – Allow 4 weeks of regrowth before getting sugared.
  • Sugar – 4-6 weeks of regrowth.
After service care
  • To avoid irritation, keep the skin clean and free of harsh lotions or products for 24-48 hrs.
  • After treatment, avoid shaving, tweezing, or picking at stray hairs or stubble to reduce ingrown hairs.
  • Slight pinking of the skin is normal and should disappear within a few hours.
  • We recommend waiting 24 hours before exposing your skin to the sun or heat (saunas and steam rooms).
  • Avoid touching, scratching or rubbing the treated area. Itching may be experienced due to increased blood flow to the area sugared.
  • Enjoy your smooth silky skin.
