Cleanness Practices
  • We are following the guidance of government and health authorities and established a protocol to ensure that our standards are being strictly adhered to and that we had the right action plans in place.
  • Sugar paste applied during a service is used only for that guest and we never double dip.
  • Biodegradable, nitrile gloves are required for handling sugar paste.
  • Both the used sugar paste and gloves are disposed of during that individual's service.
  • Our treatment tables are sprayed with a disinfectant and covered with disposable sheets between each appointment.
  • Disinfecting all tools and implements with high-level disinfectant.

We are committed to delivering excellent guest experiences, and we hope that you enjoy the safe services experience.
