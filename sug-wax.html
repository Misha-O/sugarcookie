  • Sugaring
  • Pulls the hair out in the natural direction of hair growth, taking the hair out by the root, less pain, less rashes and less ingrown hairs.
  • Hypoallergenic, produced from natural ingridients (sugar, lemon and water).
  • Requires shorter hair growth (only 1/4″ or 5mm), ensuring that the hair has less time to re-establish strong growth. This means less pain and less unsightly hair!
  • Sugar is hygienic. One ball of sugar can remove hair from a large area of the body and is applied with a gloved hand. No spatula or cotton strips are required – No double dipping.
  • Sugar is water-soluble, so clean up is a breeze.
  • Sugar is 100% natural with no chemical additives, no wax resins and no unnecessary essential oils. It is safe to use on all skin and hair types.
  • Sugar is applied at barely lukewarm temperature
  • Waxing
  • Pulls hair out against natural hair growth resulting in more hair breakage, pain, rashes and most importantly ingrown hairs.
  • Damages fine tissue cells.
  • Wax requires longer hair growth, permitting the hair to re-establish strong growth. This means more pain and more hair!
  • Waxing requires a spatula to apply over and over again and may lead to Double Dipping. This could potentially cause cross-contamination.
  • Wax requires solvents to clean it off of your skin.
  • All wax products contain resins and may not be suitable for all skin and hair types.
  • Hot wax can burn and bruise the skin.
